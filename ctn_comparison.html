Experimental Setup

We analyze how different approaches to combining cause and effect in CTN captions affect AI video generation. This investigation uses free AI video generation platforms to visualize the impact of caption structure on temporal and causal understanding.

An AI video generation platform that adapts input text for storytelling while maintaining narrative flow.

Key Findings

Aspect Concatenated Approach LLM Fusion Approach
Temporal Ordering Explicit and clear Less distinct transitions
Causal Relationship Strongly preserved Partially weakened
Video Generation More accurate scene transitions Merged scenes with less distinction
Narrative Structure Clear separation of events Smoother but less structured

Impact on AI Video Generation

The concatenated approach leads to more accurate representation of temporal sequence and causal relationships in AI-generated videos, as demonstrated by both platforms. This validates our choice of maintaining explicit temporal-causal structure in CTN captions.
