NarrativeBridge: Enhancing Video Captioning with Causal Narrative

Datasets

Our work introduces a novel benchmark for video captioning called Causal Narrative (CN) captions, generated using the Mixtral of experts LLM for popular datasets like MSR-VTT and MSVD.
